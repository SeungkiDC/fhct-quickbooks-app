Privacy Policy — FHCT Bookkeeping Automation

Effective date: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the internal software application “FHCT Bookkeeping Automation” (the “Application”), operated by Friends Healthcare Team, Inc. (“FHCT,” “we,” or “us”), handles information when it connects to QuickBooks Online.

1. Scope and purpose

The Application is used solely by FHCT for its own internal bookkeeping, financial-close automation, and accrual modeling. It is not a public or commercial product and is not distributed to, or made available to, any third party.

2. Information accessed

The Application connects to FHCT’s own QuickBooks Online company through Intuit’s authorized API using read-only access. It reads financial records belonging to FHCT — such as transactions, chart of accounts, vendors, and related bookkeeping data. It does not collect information from any individual other than through FHCT’s own accounting records.

3. How information is used

Accessed data is used exclusively to assist FHCT’s internal accounting team — for example, to reconcile bank activity, suggest account categorizations for unposted transactions, and prepare accrual schedules. The Application uses read-only access and does not modify, create, or delete data in QuickBooks Online.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share QuickBooks data with any third party. Data is processed and stored only on systems controlled by FHCT and is accessible only to authorized FHCT personnel.

5. Data security and retention

Access credentials and accessed data are stored on FHCT-controlled systems and retained only as long as needed for internal accounting purposes. FHCT may revoke the Application’s access at any time through its Intuit account.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time; the effective date above reflects the latest version.

7. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be directed to david@friendshealthcareteam.com.
